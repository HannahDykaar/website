Research

Tidal Disruption Events (TDEs)
My current research focuses on tidal disruption events (TDEs). A TDE occurs when a star gets sufficiently close to a supermassive black hole, such that the tidal forces overcome the star's self-gravity. This process results in a luminous flare observable across the electromagnetic spectrum.
